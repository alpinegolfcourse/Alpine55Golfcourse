Welcome to Alpine Golf Course

Located in the heart of Longview, TX, Alpine Golf Course offers a relaxed, welcoming course experience for every golfer from weekend walkers to seasoned members. Family-friendly, Texas proud, and open 7 days a week.

About Us

Alpine Golf Course was established with one mission in mind — to offer a fun, laid-back golf experience. We take pride in being a locally loved course, supporting our veterans, first responders, and senior golfers with discounted pricing. Come see what makes Alpine more than just a place to play.
